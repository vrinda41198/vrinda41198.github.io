About

Hello! I'm Vrinda, a Software Engineer by profession, and an unconventional observer and enthusiastic learner by nature. I have worked on a wide variety of domains including Backend Development, Big Data, Deep Learning and Data Science through various experiences and projects, which has helped me build a diverse skill set, with an ability to adapt and learn on the go. I hope to expand my network and deepen my knowledge, by collaborating with individuals and organisations, through opportunities that will allow me to develop and deliver mission-critical business and technology solutions.
